Challenge

This lab focuses on us dipping our toes into more complex block styling, including dropdowns, side content, and things more complex than a page that is just full of paragraphs and links. The previously explored "image float right" is being expanded upon to make a website of greater complexity overall and that will, with some effort, allow us to create something more modern and user friendly than just a lot of text in a row.
