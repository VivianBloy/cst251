Challenge

The challenge here is, much like lab 13, focused around recreating a website and making use of layers, overlapping elements, and applying or improving our understanding of how to control where elements are on a page, as well as effectively using the different kinds of elements that form most webpages and html files. its a cumulative review through practice in a way and, considering how lab 13 went for Polly and I, it honestly probably won't go over all that smoothly although it is absolutely reachable.

Problems

This lab actually ended up going a lot smoother than lab 13, much to our collected excitement! overall I think that, although we did have similar issues as lab 13, we managed to take what we learned from that and, after a break to collect ourselves, find a workflow through it. We encountered issues with getting content boxes to be the correct size as before, but we managed to tackle that by making a temporary border and just experimenting until we found something that worked. Also we were much more leaniant to look things up on w3schools this time around which majorly helped.

End Result

outside of the font and the background image, I'd say we were able to almost completely duplicate our referenced website, with proper spacing, use of inline elements and general organization. it was super encouraging that we quite literally found the perfect font, that being a variation on Spartan, and I feel a whole lot more confident about working with nonstandard fonts going forwards!
