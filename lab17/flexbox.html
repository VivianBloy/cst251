Challenges

The challenge found within this lab mainly focuses on fine tuned layout editing and getting more used to what the properties of "float" and "flexbox" to better arange and shift around elements on a webpage. Not only is this good practice getting our heads around each of these ideas, it also lets us test out which works best with our own personal design style and how we might combine or work with them in the future as we're creating more professional sites.
